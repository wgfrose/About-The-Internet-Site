What If It Breaks?

Well there are no two ways about it, it would be absolute chaos. The western world's and most of the eastern world's economies would crash spectacularly, the high-tech defense systems of countries like the US and UK would go offline. You would no longer be able to look up information, contact loved ones, the list goes on. I found that this video below, by BRITLAB, that lays out the main ideas for things that'll go wrong better than I could, so sit back, relax, and think at least Vogons aren't about to destroy your planet to make way for an inter-galactic bypass.
